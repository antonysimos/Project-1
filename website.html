Manchester City FC

Club History

Manchester City Football club is a football club that competes in the top flight of english football. Founded in 1880 in the city of Manchester, MCFC hosted all of its home competitions at Maine Road since 1923, before moving to the Etihad Stadium in 2003. After entering the Football League in 1892, City would not see its first major title until the FA cup in 1904, where they became the first club in Manchester to win a major honour. City currently sits at the top of the Premier League table, and are the current defending champions of the Premier League.
